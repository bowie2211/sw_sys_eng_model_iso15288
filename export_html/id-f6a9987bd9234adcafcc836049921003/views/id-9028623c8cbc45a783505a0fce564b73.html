Transition Process ()
Purpose As stated in ISO/IEC/IEEE 15288, The purpose of the Transition process is to establish a capability for a system to provide services specified by stakeholder requirements in the operational environment. Activities: Prepare for the transistion Perform the transion Manage results of transition
