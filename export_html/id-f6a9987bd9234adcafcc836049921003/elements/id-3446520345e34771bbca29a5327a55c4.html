Business or Mission Analysis Process ()
Purpose As stated in ISO/IEC/IEEE 15288: The purpose of the Business or Mission Analysis process is to define the overall strategic problem or opportunity, characterize the solution space, and determine potential solution class(es) that can address a problem or take advantage of an opportunity. Actvities: Prepare for business or mission analysis. Define the problem or opportunity space. Characterize the solution space. Evaluate alternative solution classes that span the potential solution space. Manage the Business or Mission Analysis.
