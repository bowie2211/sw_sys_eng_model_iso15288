Supply Process ()
Purpose As stated in ISO/IEC/IEEE 15288, The purpose of the Supply process is to provide an acquirer with a product or service that meets agreed requirements. Activities: Prepare for the supply. Respond to a request for supply of products or services. Establish and maintain an agreement. Execute the agreement. Deliver and support the product or service.
