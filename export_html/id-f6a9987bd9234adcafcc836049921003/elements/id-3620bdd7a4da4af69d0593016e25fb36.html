Acquistion Process ()
Purpose As stated in ISO/IEC/IEEE 15288, The purpose of the Acquisition process is to obtain a product or service in accordance with the acquirer’s requirements. Activities Prepare for the acquisition. Advertise the acquisition and select the supplier. Establish and maintain an agreement. Monitor the agreement. Accept the product or service.
