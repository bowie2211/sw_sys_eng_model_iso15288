Life Cycle Model Management Process ()
Purpose As stated in ISO/IEC/IEEE 15288, The purpose of the Life Cycle Model Management process is to define, maintain, and help ensure availability of policies, life cycle processes, life cycle models, and procedures for use by the organization with respect to the scope of ISO/IEC/IEEE 15288.
